01 — Logo
Logo Variations
Primary · Dark
Secondary · Light
Accent · Amber

Logo concept: The KM monogram pairs a geometric sans K (corporate past) with an amber M (AI/energy present). The central node at the K-midpoint references circuit design — a nod to 30 years of engineering without being literal about it. The enclosing circle suggests completeness and a global scope of work.

02 — Color
Brand Palette
Deep Navy #0D1B2A · Primary
Navy Mid #1A2F45 · Background
Amber #D4922A · Accent
Warm Cream #F5F0E8 · Light bg
Amber Light #F0B855 · Highlight

Usage — Dark

Navy as primary background. Amber for CTAs, highlights, and active states. Cream/white for body text.

Usage — Light

Cream as page background. Navy for headings and borders. Amber sparingly for emphasis only.

03 — Typography
Type System
Display · Cormorant Garamond
AI That Actually Works.
700 weight · 36–56px · Headlines, hero text, section titles
Body · DM Sans
I help SMB founders cut through the noise and put AI to work — not as a pilot project that dies in a boardroom, but as a real lever for growth.
400 weight · 15–16px · All body copy and descriptions
Caption / Label · DM Sans Light
AI Consultant · Educator · Ex-Thomson Reuters & Clarivate
300 weight · 10–12px · Labels, metadata, nav items

Pairing rationale: Cormorant Garamond gives warmth, authority, and editorial gravitas — it reads as both intelligent and human. DM Sans provides neutral legibility for body text without competing. Together they signal "seasoned professional who communicates clearly", not "startup founder or tech bro."
