Gamma was used to design and export the AI Readiness Checklist as a professional PDF lead magnet. Raw checklist content — 15 questions across four sections plus a scoring guide — was fed into Gamma, which structured and formatted it into a visually polished, branded document with a navy and gold colour scheme. The last slide includes your name, credentials, LinkedIn profile, and Calendly booking link, turning the checklist from a free download into a direct funnel entry point.
Brevo serves as the email marketing and automation backbone of the funnel. It hosts the signup form where visitors enter their email to receive the checklist, handles automatic PDF delivery upon signup, and runs the 5-email nurture sequence over 18 days that builds trust and moves subscribers toward booking a discovery call. It replaces the need for a paid CRM at this stage of your consulting practice.
Bitly was brought in purely to solve a practical problem — the Brevo signup form URL was too long and unsightly to place in your LinkedIn About section and Featured section. A custom branded short link, bit.ly/ai-readiness-india, was created so the CTA in your LinkedIn profile looks clean and intentional rather than like a raw system-generated URL.
Calendly was set up as the booking engine at the bottom of the funnel. It connects to your Google Calendar, shows available slots in IST, and allows warm prospects to book a free 30-minute AI Strategy Discovery Call without any back-and-forth email coordination. The link — calendly.com/krishnamohn/30min — was embedded in Email 5 of the Brevo nurture sequence, the last slide of the Gamma checklist PDF, and your website contact section.
Formspree powers the contact form on your personal website. Rather than building a backend to handle form submissions, Formspree acts as the middleman — when a visitor fills in the contact form on your site and hits Send, the message is routed directly to your email via Formspree's servers. It required no server-side code, making it the simplest way to add a functional contact form to a static HTML website.
